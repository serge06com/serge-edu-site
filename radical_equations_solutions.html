Grade 12 Algebra – Radical Equations (Solutions)

  1. Solve for x: √(x + 5) = 3
    Solution: Square both sides: x + 5 = 9 → x = 4
  2. Solve: √(2x - 3) + 4 = 7
    Solution: √(2x - 3) = 3 → square both sides: 2x - 3 = 9 → 2x = 12 → x = 6

Question 1: Solve: \( x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0 \)

Step 1: Factor the quadratic: \( (x - 2)(x - 3) \)

Step 2: Set each factor equal to 0: \( x = 2 \) or \( x = 3 \)

Final Answer: \( x = 2 \) or \( x = 3 \)

Question 2: Solve: \( x^2 + 4x + 4 = 0 \)

Step 1: Perfect square: \( (x + 2)^2 = 0 \)

Final Answer: \( x = -2 \)

Question 3: Solve: \( x^2 - 9 = 0 \)

Step 1: Difference of squares: \( (x - 3)(x + 3) = 0 \)

Final Answer: \( x = 3 \) or \( x = -3 \)

Question 4: Solve: \( 2x^2 - 7x + 3 = 0 \)

Step 1: Use quadratic formula:

\( x = \frac{7 \pm \sqrt{(-7)^2 - 4(2)(3)}}{2(2)} = \frac{7 \pm \sqrt{49 - 24}}{4} = \frac{7 \pm 5}{4} \)

Final Answer: \( x = 3 \) or \( x = \frac{1}{2} \)

Question 5: Solve: \( x^2 + x - 12 = 0 \)

Step 1: Factor: \( (x + 4)(x - 3) = 0 \)

Final Answer: \( x = -4 \) or \( x = 3 \)

Question 6: Solve: \( 4x^2 - 9 = 0 \)

Step 1: Use difference of squares: \( (2x - 3)(2x + 3) = 0 \)

Final Answer: \( x = \frac{3}{2} \) or \( x = -\frac{3}{2} \)

Question 7: Solve: \( x^2 + 2x + 1 = 0 \)

Question 8: Solve: \( 3x^2 + 10x + 7 = 0 \)

\( x = \frac{-10 \pm \sqrt{100 - 84}}{6} = \frac{-10 \pm \sqrt{16}}{6} = \frac{-10 \pm 4}{6} \)

Final Answer: \( x = -1 \) or \( x = -\frac{7}{3} \)

Grade 12 Number Patterns - Arithmetic Sequences Solutions

1. Find the 15th term of the arithmetic sequence: 4, 7, 10, 13, ...

Given: a₁ = 4, d = 3, n = 15

Formula for nth term: aₙ = a₁ + (n - 1)d

Calculate:
a₁₅ = 4 + (15 - 1) × 3
    = 4 + 14 × 3
    = 4 + 42
    = 46

Answer: The 15th term is 46.

2. Determine the common difference of the sequence: 5, 11, 17, 23, ...

Given terms: 5, 11, 17, 23

Calculate common difference d:
d = 11 - 5 = 6

Answer: The common difference is 6.

3. Find the sum of the first 20 terms of the sequence: 2, 5, 8, 11, ...

Given: a₁ = 2, d = 3, n = 20

Sum formula: Sₙ = (n/2) × [2a₁ + (n - 1)d]

Calculate:
S₂₀ = (20/2) × [2 × 2 + (20 - 1) × 3]
    = 10 × [4 + 19 × 3]
    = 10 × [4 + 57]
    = 10 × 61
    = 610

Answer: The sum of the first 20 terms is 610.

5. Calculate the sum of the first 15 terms of the sequence: 1, 4, 7, 10, ...

Given: a₁ = 1, d = 3, n = 15

S₁₅ = (n/2)[2a₁ + (n - 1)d]
    = (15/2)[2 × 1 + (15 - 1) × 3]
    = 7.5[2 + 42]
    = 7.5 × 44
    = 330

Answer: The sum is 330.

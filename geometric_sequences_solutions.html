Grade 12 Algebra – Geometric Sequences (Solutions)

2.1 Find the 6th term of the sequence: 3, 6, 12, 24, ...
Solution: Here, first term \(a = 3\), common ratio \(r = 2\).
\(a_6 = a \times r^{6-1} = 3 \times 2^5 = 3 \times 32 = 96\).

2.2 Determine the common ratio of the sequence: 5, 15, 45, 135, ...
Solution: \(\displaystyle r = \frac{15}{5} = 3\).

2.3 Find the sum of the first 5 terms of the sequence: 2, 4, 8, 16, ...
Solution: \(a=2, r=2, n=5\).
Sum \(S_n = a \frac{r^n - 1}{r - 1} = 2 \times \frac{2^5 - 1}{2 - 1} = 2 \times \frac{32 - 1}{1} = 2 \times 31 = 62\).

2.4 Find the 8th term of the geometric sequence: 1, 3, 9, 27, ...
Solution: \(a=1, r=3\).
\(a_8 = 1 \times 3^{7} = 3^7 = 2187\).

2.5 Calculate the sum of the first 4 terms: 7, 14, 28, 56, ...
Solution: \(a=7, r=2, n=4\).
\(S_4 = 7 \times \frac{2^4 - 1}{2 - 1} = 7 \times \frac{16 - 1}{1} = 7 \times 15 = 105\).

2.6 Find the 10th term of the sequence: 81, 27, 9, 3, ...
Solution: \(a=81, r=\frac{27}{81} = \frac{1}{3}\).
\(a_{10} = 81 \times \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^{9} = 81 \times \frac{1}{19683} = \frac{81}{19683} = \frac{1}{243}\).

2.7 Calculate the sum of the first 6 terms of the sequence: 5, 10, 20, 40, ...
Solution: \(a=5, r=2, n=6\).
\(S_6 = 5 \times \frac{2^6 - 1}{2 - 1} = 5 \times (64 - 1) = 5 \times 63 = 315\).

2.8 Find the 7th term of the geometric sequence where \(a=2\), \(r=3\).
Solution:
\(a_7 = 2 \times 3^{6} = 2 \times 729 = 1458\).

2.9 Determine the sum of the first 3 terms: 9, 3, 1, ...
Solution: \(a=9, r=\frac{3}{9} = \frac{1}{3}, n=3\).
\(S_3 = 9 \times \frac{(1/3)^3 - 1}{(1/3) - 1} = 9 \times \frac{\frac{1}{27} - 1}{\frac{1}{3} - 1} = 9 \times \frac{-\frac{26}{27}}{-\frac{2}{3}} = 9 \times \frac{26}{27} \times \frac{3}{2} = 13.0\).

2.10 Find the 5th term of the sequence: 16, 8, 4, 2, ...
Solution: \(a=16, r=\frac{8}{16} = \frac{1}{2}\).
\(a_5 = 16 \times \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{4} = 16 \times \frac{1}{16} = 1\).

2.11 Calculate the sum of the first 8 terms: 1, 4, 16, 64, ...
Solution: \(a=1, r=4, n=8\).
\(S_8 = 1 \times \frac{4^8 - 1}{4 - 1} = \frac{65536 - 1}{3} = \frac{65535}{3} = 21845\).

2.12 Find the 4th term of the geometric sequence: 5, 15, 45, ...
Solution: \(a=5, r=3\).
\(a_4 = 5 \times 3^{3} = 5 \times 27 = 135\).

2.13 Determine the sum of the first 10 terms: 2, 6, 18, ...

2.14 Find the 3rd term of the sequence where \(a=7\), \(r=0.5\).
Solution:
\(a_3 = 7 \times 0.5^{2} = 7 \times 0.25 = 1.75\).

2.15 Calculate the sum of the first 5 terms of: 8, 4, 2, 1, ...
Solution: \(a=8, r=0.5, n=5\).
\(S_5 = 8 \times \frac{0.5^5 - 1}{0.5 - 1} = 8 \times \frac{0.03125 - 1}{-0.5} = 8 \times \frac{-0.96875}{-0.5} = 8 \times 1.9375 = 15.5\).

Grade 12 Algebra – Arithmetic Sequences (Solutions)

2.6 Find the 15th term of the arithmetic sequence: 4, 7, 10, 13, ...
\(a_1 = 4, d = 3\)
\(a_{15} = a_1 + (15 - 1)d = 4 + 14 \times 3 = 46\)

2.7 Determine the common difference of the sequence: 5, 11, 17, 23, ...
\(d = 11 - 5 = 6\)

2.8 Find the sum of the first 20 terms of the sequence: 2, 5, 8, 11, ...
\(a_1 = 2, d = 3, n = 20\)
Sum \(S_n = \frac{n}{2} (2a_1 + (n-1)d) = \frac{20}{2} (4 + 57) = 10 \times 61 = 610\)

2.9 Find the 10th term of the sequence: 3, 6, 9, 12, ...
\(a_1=3, d=3\)
\(a_{10} = 3 + (10-1) \times 3 = 3 + 27 = 30\)

2.10 Find the sum of the first 15 terms: 1, 4, 7, 10, ...
\(a_1=1, d=3, n=15\)
\(S_{15} = \frac{15}{2} (2 \times 1 + (15-1) \times 3) = \frac{15}{2} (2 + 42) = \frac{15}{2} \times 44 = 330\)

2.11 Find the 20th term of the sequence: 10, 8, 6, 4, ...

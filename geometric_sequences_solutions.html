\(S_n = a \times \frac{r^n - 1}{r - 1}\)

\[ S_3 = 9 \times \frac{\left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^3 - 1}{\frac{1}{3} - 1} = 9 \times \frac{\frac{1}{27} - 1}{\frac{1}{3} - 1} = 9 \times \frac{-\frac{26}{27}}{-\frac{2}{3}} \]
Step 4: Simplify fractions:
\[ = 9 \times \frac{26}{27} \times \frac{3}{2} = 9 \times \frac{26 \times 3}{27 \times 2} = 9 \times \frac{78}{54} = 9 \times \frac{13}{9} = \boxed{13} \]

Grade 12 Algebra - Exponential Equations (Solutions)

1.
Given: \( 2^x = 8 \)
Write 8 as a power of 2: \( 8 = 2^3 \)
So: \( 2^x = 2^3 \Rightarrow x = 3 \)
2.
Given: \( 3^{x+1} = 81 \)
Write 81 as a power of 3: \( 81 = 3^4 \)
So: \( x + 1 = 4 \Rightarrow x = 3 \)
3.
Given: \( 5^{2x} = 125 \)
Write 125 as a power of 5: \( 125 = 5^3 \)
So: \( 2x = 3 \Rightarrow x = 1.5 \)
4.
Given: \( 4^{x-2} = 1 \)
Since any base to the power of 0 is 1: \( 4^0 = 1 \)
So: \( x - 2 = 0 \Rightarrow x = 2 \)
5.
Given: \( 9^x = \frac{1}{3} \)
Convert both to base 3: \( 9 = 3^2 \), \( \frac{1}{3} = 3^{-1} \)
So: \( 3^{2x} = 3^{-1} \Rightarrow 2x = -1 \Rightarrow x = -\frac{1}{2} \)
6.
Given: \( 2^{3x} = 16 \)
Write 16 as a power of 2: \( 16 = 2^4 \)
So: \( 3x = 4 \Rightarrow x = \frac{4}{3} \)
7.
Given: \( 7^{x+1} = 49 \)
Write 49 as a power of 7: \( 49 = 7^2 \)
So: \( x + 1 = 2 \Rightarrow x = 1 \)
8.
Given: \( 3^{2x-1} = 27 \)
Write 27 as a power of 3: \( 27 = 3^3 \)
So: \( 2x - 1 = 3 \Rightarrow 2x = 4 \Rightarrow x = 2 \)
9.
Given: \( 5^{x+2} = 625 \)
Write 625 as a power of 5: \( 625 = 5^4 \)
So: \( x + 2 = 4 \Rightarrow x = 2 \)
10.
Given: \( 10^x = 0.001 \)
Convert 0.001 to a power of 10: \( 0.001 = 10^{-3} \)
So: \( x = -3 \)
11.
Given: \( 3^x = 1 \)
Since any number to the power 0 is 1: \( 3^0 = 1 \)
So: \( x = 0 \)
12.

Write 36 as \( 6^2 \)
So: \( x - 2 = 2 \Rightarrow x = 4 \)
13.

14.

15.
Given: \( 4^x = 0.25 \)
Write 0.25 as \( \frac{1}{4} = 4^{-1} \)
So: \( x = -1 \)
16.
Given: \( 2^{x+2} = 32 \)
Write 32 as \( 2^5 \)
So: \( x + 2 = 5 \Rightarrow x = 3 \)
17.
Given: \( 3^{2x+1} = 243 \)
Write 243 as \( 3^5 \)
So: \( 2x + 1 = 5 \Rightarrow 2x = 4 \Rightarrow x = 2 \)
18.
Given: \( 9^x = 81 \)
Write both in base 3: \( 9 = 3^2, 81 = 3^4 \)
So: \( (3^2)^x = 3^4 \Rightarrow 3^{2x} = 3^4 \Rightarrow 2x = 4 \Rightarrow x = 2 \)

So: \( x = -1 \)
